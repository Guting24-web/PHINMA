Corporate Social Responsibility

PHINMA, through its foundation and strategic business units, aims to make lives better for Filipino families and communities through community service and volunteer engagement, enhancing the effectiveness of its programs.

The PHINMA Foundation, Inc. (PFI) was established in 1989 to improve lives by harnessing resources and creating a culture of service. It aims to mold future leaders, provide education and leadership programs, create meaningful change in Filipino families, and open volunteer opportunities. PFI prioritizes education programs, preserving culture, promoting values, and protecting the environment. The flagship program, PNS, was established in 2006 and has produced hundreds of graduates in education, accountancy, and engineering. PFI partners with regional science high schools and universities across 10 regions. Employees are encouraged to contribute through company-led CSR projects and the PHINMA Hero program, fostering volunteerism and sustainability. PFI also provides financial assistance to various causes, including hunger and poverty, youth empowerment, and culture and arts promotion.
