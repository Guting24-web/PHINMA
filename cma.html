College of Management and Accountancy

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

The Bachelor of Science in Accountancy trains students in accounting and auditing. It prepares its students for careers in accounting firms, financial institutions, and financial departments of the government or private companies. The graduates of this program are qualified to take the Certified Accounting Technician, the Certified Management Accountant, Certified Internal Auditor, as well as the Certified Public Accountant exams, the passing of which makes them highly in demand and competitive.

Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System (BSAIS)

The Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System (BSAIS) is a four-year program that combines the concepts and principles of business accounting and information technology. Graduates of BSAIS may be part of the business process outsourcing industry, practice accounting in an institution, or work in industries that rely on management information systems and entrepreneurial planning solutions. They are also qualified to take the Certified Accounting Technician exam.

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

The Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management aims to develop students to be qualified personnel and entrepreneurs in the tourism industry. Covering various areas in the travel and tourism industry, the program emphasizes managerial theory and the hands-on application of this.
