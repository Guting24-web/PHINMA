College of Allied Health Science

Bachelor of Science in Medical Laboratory Science

This program trains students to examine body tissues to aid in the diagnosis of diseases. Students are prepared for careers in the hospital and clinical settings where they can conduct and supervise medical tests and manage laboratory procedures.

Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

This program offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines theory with practical experience, preparing students for success in business planning, financial management, marketing, and innovation, fostering creativity and resilience.
